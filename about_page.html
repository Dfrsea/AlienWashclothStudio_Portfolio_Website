About Alien Washcloth

Hi, I’m the solo developer behind Alien Washcloth. I make small games built around simple mechanics, with an emphasis on feel and unexpected ideas. The goal is to create games that are easy to pick up, but slightly odd in a way that sticks with you.

What Does "Alien Washcloth" Mean?

The name doesn’t mean anything specific. It’s just two unrelated words I put together because I liked how strange it sounded. That idea carries into my games too: simple concepts with a weird or unexpected twist.

Skills & Tools

I’ve worked with a range of game engines, from Scratch and Love2D to Unity and Godot. Right now, my engine of choice is Godot.

I’m also learning pixel art so I can eventually make more of my own visuals instead of relying on asset packs.
