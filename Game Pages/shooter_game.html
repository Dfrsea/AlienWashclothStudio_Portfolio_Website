Overview

Don't let its simplicity fool you! This fast-paced shooter is weirdly addictive, perfect for action enthusiasts. Complete with enemies, score, and even health upgrades, this game may be small, but it's simmering with fun!

Controls:

  • WASD for movement
  • Mouse to aim
  • Left Mouse Button to shoot

Goal:

Surive as long as possible! Dodge & shoot enemies, avoid getting hit, and if you're running low, try to find a health upgrade!

Features:

  • Enemies that spawn randomly from the edges of the screen.
  • Score and health systems
  • Randomly spawning health upgrades
